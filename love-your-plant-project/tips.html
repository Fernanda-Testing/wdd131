🌟 TOP 5 UNIVERSAL TIPS FOR PLANT CARE 1. Not all plants need the same amount of water 💧 👉 Overwatering kills more plants than underwatering. Tip: Touch the soil before watering — if it’s still moist, wait. If it’s dry, water gently. 2. Provide the right light — not always direct sunlight ☀️ 👉 Each plant has different light needs: low, medium, or bright. Tip: Watch the leaves — if they stretch toward the light, they need more; if they burn, they’re getting too much. 3. Use a pot with good drainage 🪴 👉 Roots need air and should never sit in stagnant water. Tip: Make sure the pot has drainage holes and don’t let water collect in the saucer. 4. Clean the leaves and inspect your plant 🌿 👉 Dust blocks light and clogs leaf pores. Tip: Wipe leaves with a damp cloth or give them a light shower. Check for pests, dry leaves, or unusual spots. 5. Know your plant and observe it regularly 👀 👉 Plants “talk” through signs like drooping, color changes, or slow growth. Tip: Do a little research about your plant, but also trust your observation to adjust care.
