Site Plan of Fernanda Souza

Site Name: The “I Want to Take Care of My Plant” Club

This name was chosen because this page is aimed at people who are interested in learning how to care for their plants.

Site Purpose:

The "I Want to Take Care of My Plants” Club is aweb page for helps people interested in plants who have questions about their care and the difficulties of growing them. I share the advantages that is to have in our enviroment plants. And I aim for it to be a space where suggestions and questions can be made so I can offer the best to those interested."

Scenarios:

  • How often should I water my plants?
  • How to identify if a plant is indoor or outdoor?
  • How to identify if a plant is indoor or outdoor?
  • Does it help if I talk to my plants?
